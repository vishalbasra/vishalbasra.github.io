What is knife?

**[Knife](https://docs.chef.io/knife.html)** is what let's us talk to the Chef server.

## Knife Handy Commands


| Acronym        | Explanation  |
| ------------- |:-------------:|
| `knife node list`      | Show all nodes |
| `knife node show <nodename>`      | Show the details of the node-name |
